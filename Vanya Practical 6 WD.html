Mr. Ratan Tata

Ratan Naval Tata, a towering figure in Indian industry and the chairman emeritus of Tata Group, passed away on Wednesday night at the age of 86. His death marks the end of an era, with an outpouring of tributes from leaders across the business and political spectrum, who hailed him as a true visionary who left an indelible mark on Indian industry. He was an inspiring leader, known for his vision, innovation, and philanthropy.
